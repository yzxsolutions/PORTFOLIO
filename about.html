About Us

YZX Solutions, based in Bangalore, is a beacon of innovation in the IT industry. Founded by visionaries Abhinav, Alan, Fayad, and Rizwan, our company is dedicated to delivering cutting-edge technology solutions that ensure client satisfaction.
